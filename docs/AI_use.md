# AI Usage Policy

## Overview

AI-assisted tools are permitted within xLaDe. Contributors may use AI for assistance in coding, documentation, refactoring, or related tasks.

## Responsibility

Contributors remain fully responsible for all submitted work, regardless of AI assistance.

Final authorship and accountability belong to the contributor submitting the work.

## Disclosure

Disclosure of AI usage is completely optional. Noone is required to disclose the use of AI mandatorily. Still voluntary disclosure of the tools or models used is appreciated.

## Understanding Requirement

Contributors should understand the code or documentation they submit and be able to explain its purpose and behavior if needed.

Blind or unreviewed usage of AI-generated output is discouraged.

## Research Integrity

AI-generated content should not replace critical reasoning, verification, or human review, especially in research-oriented areas of the project.

## Prohibited Practices

- Using AI blindly anywhere
- Submitting AI-generated code without understanding its behavior
- Using AI to change or translate core modules to other languages without understanding
- Relying on AI models with poor coding and reasoning capabilities
- Weak local LLMs with low-quality output

# Note

The policy will be regularly updated in accordance with developments in AI, contributors and discussions.
